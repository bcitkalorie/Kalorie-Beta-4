English Breakfast

Ingridients:

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Cup Mushrooms
  • ¼ Cup Baked Beans
  • 2 Leafs Lettuce
  • 1 Ripe Tomato
  • 1 Sweet Potato
  • Salt - to taste
  • Pepper - to taste
  • Olive oil

Intructions:

  • Preheat oven to 300ºC - 420ºF
  • Slice the tomato to ¼ in slices
  • Cut mushrooms in half
  • Slice the Sweet Potato to wedges, seasons with salt, peppers and Olive Oil
  • Bake the Sweet Potato for 30 minutes, flip half-way
  • In a medium heat scillet, sauté the mushrooms until tender with olive oil
  • Sear the tomato and get some browning
  • Fried the eggs to your desire
  • Serve hot with salt and pepper, garnish with beked beans and lettuce
